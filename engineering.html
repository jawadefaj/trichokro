Engineering Excellence

Precision engineered for durability, safety, and efficiency on Bangladeshi roads.

Aerodynamic Design

Our unique retractable hood design significantly reduces air drag coefficient compared to traditional flat-front rickshaws. This optimization leads to a 15% increase in range per charge and better stability at higher speeds (up to 50 km/h).

Hydraulic Braking

Safety is paramount. We replaced the standard mechanical drum brakes with a triple-disc hydraulic braking system (Hand, Foot, Handle). This reduces braking distance by 40% and eliminates the risk of brake fade in heavy traffic.

Truss Chassis

Validated through ANSYS load simulations, our tubular truss chassis distributes stress evenly. It is 35% lighter than traditional angular chassis yet supports 80% more load (up to 700kg), preventing structural fatigue.

LiFePO4 Technology

We utilize Lithium Iron Phosphate batteries which offer 3000+ charge cycles (vs 500 for Lead-Acid). They are non-toxic, non-explosive, and provide consistent power output regardless of charge level.
