Trambulance ‚Äî Three-Wheeled Ambulance

TriChokro Trambulance is a fully electric, three-wheeled ambulance designed for the unique needs of Bangladesh. Its compact size, maneuverability, and low running cost make it ideal for rapid response in busy city streets or rural roads.

Key Features:

  • All-Electric Powertrain: Zero emissions and quiet operation.
  • Medical-Grade Interior: Equipped for patient safety and comfort.
  • Stretcher Space: Designed to carry one patient and one attendant.
  • Easy Access: Wide doors for quick loading and unloading.
  • Urban & Rural Ready: Compact build to navigate traffic and narrow lanes.
  • Low Operating Cost: Minimal charging and maintenance expenses.
