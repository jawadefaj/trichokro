The Trambulance.
Saving Lives, Sustainably.

TriChokro Trambulance is a fully electric, three-wheeled ambulance designed for the unique needs of Bangladesh. Its compact size allows it to reach patients where traditional ambulances cannot.

Engineering Excellence

We combined real-world driver benefits with robust product features. Every component is chosen for durability and efficiency.

Aerodynamic Hood

Retractable roof design lowers air drag and adapts to weather instantly.

Hydraulic Disk Brakes

Triple setup (Hand, Foot, Handle) gives driver full control.

Smart Dashboard

Digital speed, state-of-charge, and trip info keep you in control.

The Crisis

Why current ambulances
fail us.

In Puran Dhaka, traffic paralyzes large vans. In rural areas, muddy roads are impassable. Traditional ambulances cost 4-5 Million BDT and are often unavailable.

High Cost: Unbearably high for rural communities.

Limited Access: Cannot enter narrow 6m radius roads.

Incoordination: Fragmented contact numbers, no real-time data.
