01.

The Current Crisis

Unsafe electric rickshaws have flooded Dhaka's roads, causing accidents and environmental hazards. We identified three critical failure points.

Safety Hazards

  • Non-existent braking systems
  • High center of gravity (tipping risk)
  • Poor suspension causing injury

Engineering Faults

  • High aerodynamic drag
  • Low load capacity wheels
  • Inferior structural materials

Environmental Impact

  • Non-recyclable toxic batteries
  • Industrial waste from imports
  • Noise pollution from old engines
02.

The Solution

Engineering excellence meets local innovation.

BUET Innovated

McPherson Suspension

Superior ride comfort and stability compared to traditional leaf springs.

Disc Brakes

Modern hydraulic disc brakes replacing dangerous rubber pad brakes.

Aerodynamic Design

Optimized hood and body to reduce drag and increase battery efficiency.

Eco-Sustainable

Recyclable components and battery technology that is not harmful to the environment.
