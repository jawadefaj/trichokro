A World Drowning in Waste

Environmental Catastrophe

Millions of non-biodegradable tires accumulated in landfills. Burning tires releases +37% CO and +24% SO2, creating toxic air pollution.

The Bangladesh Crisis

Approximately 150,000 tons of waste tires generated annually. Widespread dumping and burning intensify local air and water pollution.

Infrastructure Failure

Conventional asphalt fails prematurely under heat and heavy loads, leading to constant maintenance and inefficient infrastructure.

The TribOriGen Solution

From Waste to
High-Performance

We utilize a circular approach using Ground Tire Rubber (GTR) to produce elastomers that are both durable and self-healing.

1

Shredding

Tires are shredded into chips using a steel-capable unit.

2

Grinding

Chips ground to 20-40 mesh. Magnetic separators remove steel.

3

Devulcanization

Pan milling applies shear to break sulfur crosslinks (controlled).

4

Activation

Mechanochemical activation creates reactive rubber powder.
